STUDY SELECTION
INPUT: 
Use Deliverable D3 from Step 2 to retrieve all articles. For each of them apply PROCESS below.
PROCESS:
Carefully check each study against inclusion and exclusion criteria defined in step 1: 
1)	Only include articles meeting the Inclusion criteria: [study designs], [populations], [outcomes], [settings].
2)	Remove deduplicate abstracts, PDFs, and PubMed results (match by DOI/PMID/title+year+first author).
3)	Remove articles meeting the exclusion criteria: [languages], [animal/in vitro unless mechanistic], [case reports if excluded], etc.
4)	Maintain a selection log with reasons for exclusion at abstract (or full-text when available) screening.
DELIVERABLE: The list of selected papers identified by DOI or PMID or title+year+first author where appropriate
