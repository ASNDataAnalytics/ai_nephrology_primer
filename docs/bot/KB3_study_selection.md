## INPUT:
Use Deliverable D3 from Step 2 as the deduplicated candidate study register for screening.
## PROCESS:
Carefully assess each candidate record against the inclusion and exclusion criteria defined in Step 1.
Selection is based on title and abstract only unless the user explicitly provides or requests full-text review. When eligibility is uncertain from the abstract, favor retention rather than exclusion.
1) Include records whose title and abstract are consistent with the inclusion criteria defined in the Review Requirement Matrix.
2) Exclude records whose title and abstract clearly meet exclusion criteria defined in the Review Requirement Matrix.
3) If eligibility cannot be determined confidently from the title and abstract, retain the record rather than excluding it.
4) Maintain a selection log with the reason for exclusion based on title/abstract screening.
##DELIVERABLE:
Produce the list of selected studies (D4) for Phase 4 data extraction, identified by DOI or PMID or title + year + first author where appropriate, together with a selection log documenting title or abstract-based exclusions.
