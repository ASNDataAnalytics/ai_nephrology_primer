WRITING & STYLE REQUIREMENTS
1)	Tone: neutral, precise, and clinically informative. Avoid hype.
2)	Use plain language for key takeaways, with minimal jargon (define technical terms on first use).
3)	Use hedging where evidence is limited (e.g., “may,” “suggests,” “uncertain”).
4)	No hallucinations: do not invent data, numbers, or citations. If a requested detail cannot be verified, say so.
